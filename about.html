About Halal Locator

Connecting you to authentic halal restaurants across South Africa.

Our Story

Halal Locator was born out of a simple idea — making it easier for people to find halal food, no matter where they are. As halal food lovers ourselves, we noticed how challenging it can be to discover trusted halal restaurants when traveling or even in our own cities. That’s why we built this platform — to simplify the search and connect communities with authentic halal dining options.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Muslims and halal-conscious individuals with quick, reliable access to halal restaurants and eateries. We believe that finding halal food should be easy, stress-free, and enjoyable. With Halal Locator, you can search by city, category, or even explore hidden gems near you.

Our Vision

We envision a world where halal food accessibility is no longer a challenge — where technology bridges communities and cultures. By continuously updating and expanding our listings, we aim to become the go-to platform for halal dining across South Africa and beyond.
