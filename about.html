About

Halaal Restaurant Finder SA helps you discover halaal dining options across South Africa. It is built with Google Maps and Places, and is optimized for a fast, mobile-friendly experience.

Disclaimer: Results come from Google Places and may not always reflect current certification. Please verify halaal status directly with the venue.

Our mission

Make it easy for anyone to find halaal food options wherever they are in South Africa—quickly, reliably, and on any device.
